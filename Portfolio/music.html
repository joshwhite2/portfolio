As a graduate of the Memorial University of Newfoundland's school of music with a focus on composition, and a diploma of audio engineering from OIART, I have a developed a deep passion for writing and producing music. Below are a number of projects I have written under different pseudonyms, performed and produced in my home studio.


Shadow's Hand - Nature Found a New Way to Scare Me (2021)

Shadow's Hand was a project of instrumentals with live instruments and a focus on world rhythms. Somber and moody, it was recorded in my basement during the winter of 2020/2021. Listen Here

Great Mess - In Twos (2018)

Great Mess was a solo electronic project. Recorded in 2016 and mixed in Boom Box studios in toronto, In twos was released as four pairs of two songs, all spaced two months apart. This album explores other worldly textures with a strong focus on duality Listen Here

GEM - Seeing Stars (2015)

Gem was a synth pop band consisting of myself and my friend Emily Locke. Emily wrote the lyrics and melodies while I wrote the orchestrations and arrangements. This was the first album I produced and mixed Listen Here
